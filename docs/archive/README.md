# Archive

Historical plans and research that have been **implemented**, **deferred**, or **absorbed** into the current codebase. Kept for traceability — not load-bearing for present work.

## How things land here

- **Plans** with `STATUS: DONE` / `IMPLEMENTED` / `DEFERRED` get moved here once the work is merged or the rationale for shelving is recorded.
- **Research** docs get moved here when their findings are folded into code/design and the doc is no longer the source of truth (it's a snapshot of the research moment, not a living reference).
- One-off content (tactics, growth campaigns, expired drafts) gets **deleted**, not archived.

## What lives in `docs/` (not here)

- `docs/when-to-use-what.md`, `docs/how-it-works.md`, `docs/orchestrator.md`, etc. — living guides
- `docs/reference/` — current reference material kept current
- `docs/plans/` — **active** plans only (anything DONE/DEFERRED moves here)
- `docs/research/` — **active** research where conclusions are still being absorbed

If you find yourself reading something here and wishing it were in `docs/`, that's a signal the topic is live again — promote it back, don't dual-maintain.
